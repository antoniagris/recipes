Lucky Charms Soup

as pictured above, this soup will conjure some green friends

This is an old family recipe that's been passed down for over 100 years. I remember my great-grandmother Gillfish making this on the stove. Which was weird, because you don't need to cook this.

You Will Need:

  • A rice
  • Jeremy
  • 15 drops of french dressing
  • some milk (cow)
  • walnuts
  • 2 cups of Lucky Charms cereal

    • Here's how to make this lovely snack!

    1. pray
    2. get up
    3. turn on blender
    4. turn off blender
    5. touch toes
    6. get a bowl made of glass out of the cabinet
    7. pour Lucky Charms into bowl
    8. sniff
    9. turn up the music
    10. pray again
    11. get out milk and pour it in and you are done!
